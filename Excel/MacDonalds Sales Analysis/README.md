# 🍔 **McDonald's Sales Analysis Dashboard**
### 📊 *Excel | Data Analytics | Business Insights*  
**by Vaishnavi — WsCubeTech Projects**

---

## 🧩 **Project Overview**
This project analyzes **McDonald’s sales data** to uncover actionable insights into customer preferences, sales patterns, and category-wise performance.

Using **Excel’s advanced tools** — *Power Query*, *Power Pivot*, *DAX Measures*, and *interactive dashboards* — this project provides a clear picture of how sales behave across different months, time periods, and categories to drive **data-backed business decisions**.

---

## 🎯 **Objectives**
- Identify **top-performing categories** and **bestselling items**  
- Understand **time-based order behavior**  
- Analyze **monthly growth and sales trends**  
- Compare **weekday vs weekend revenue**  
- Build a **visually engaging interactive dashboard**

---

## 🧠 **Business Questions**
- 💵 What is the **total sales revenue** by category?  
- 🍔 Which **menu item** sells the most?  
- 📈 How does **revenue trend month-to-month**?  
- 🧾 What is the **average order value**?  
- 🕒 How do **orders vary by time of day**?  
- 🏆 Which are the **top 5 revenue-generating items**?  
- 📅 How do **sales differ on weekends vs weekdays**?

---

## 💡 **Key Insights**
- 🍔 **Burgers** are the highest-selling category and major revenue contributor.  
- 🌞 **Afternoon hours** account for nearly **49% of total orders**.  
- 📅 **Weekend revenue** slightly surpasses weekdays, indicating higher footfall.  
- 📈 **March** shows a **+7.34% growth**, recovering from February’s dip.  
- 🧮 **Average Order Value (AOV):** $2.25 — frequent low-value orders dominate.  
- 🔝 **Top 5 menu items** contribute to over **60% of total sales**.

---

## 🧮 **Measures (DAX Calculations)**
| **Measure Name** | **Formula / Logic** | **Purpose** |
|------------------|----------------------|--------------|
| **Total Sales** | `SUM(Price)` | Total revenue generated |
| **Total Orders** | `COUNT(Order_ID)` | Number of orders placed |
| **Average Order Value** | `[Total Sales] / [Total Orders]` | Customer spending per order |
| **Monthly Growth %** | `DIVIDE([This Month] - [Prev Month], [Prev Month])` | Monthly performance trend |
| **Weekend vs Weekday Revenue** | Based on `DayName` | Compare weekday/weekend sales |
| **Revenue by Category** | `SUM(Price)` grouped by Category | Category contribution to total sales |
